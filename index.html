The Trump Tweet Translator is a public-use app to translate real tweets made by Donald Trump.

First, start the app; then, get a random tweet by clicking the "Get New Tweet" button. Then, select the language you would like to translate the tweet into the first time around and press the "Translate!" button.

After the language you picked, the tweet will be translated into Chinese, then back to English for your viewing pleasure! Hope you enjoy!
